About the church

The sent leader of this House is Pastor Austin E-Ben. He is a man who is fully reliant on the Holy Spirit. After giving his life to Christ, he jumped into the work of ministry. In a short time, he had served as a Youth Pastor, Teen Pastor and Assistant Pastor with a complete dedication to God and His work. Fully consecrated to God, he was trained under several leaders till late 2013. It was at this time he met his spiritual father; Bishop Adol-Paul Obinwaogu, who God used to commission him into ministry.

Church history

In the Summer of 2014, a Bible study meeting started at St Giles Parish Hall, and on Sunday 26th October of the same year, the Sunday services began. Since then, God has strengthened and increased His work through His servant. Ever since God sent His servant, God has always been with the Church and the Power of God has been present from the very first day.
