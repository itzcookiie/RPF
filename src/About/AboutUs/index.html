About us

Who is Redeemed Pillar of Fire?

Redeemed Pillar of Fire Church, also known as RPF, is an End time church preparing men and women of God for the last outpouring of His Spirit upon the church. We are family-orientated church that seeks to support its members in every area to have a prosperous life, fulfil their callings and walk as well as hear from the spirit. This church is called to be like the early church of the apostles rooted in Acts 2: 43-47.

From praise and worship to warfare prayers, every soul is nurtured in all areas of Godly Spirituality so that they are ready and equipped for the tribulations of the end times. The only aim is Heaven and the only way to get there is through Holiness and righteousness. Please come and join us on the journey of knowing Jesus Christ by attending any of our services or contacting us via telephone or our social media platforms.
